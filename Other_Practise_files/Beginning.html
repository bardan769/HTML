How to make Tea

Requirement

  • Tea
  • water
  • sugar
  • Milk
  • Tea Masala

Recipe

  1. First take a mixer of water and milk in bowl and boils it
  2. Now add sugar to the mixer
  3. After adding sugar add Tea to it
  4. Add Tea masala according to the taste
  5. Making of tea masala:
    • First chop a garic and lasun
    • Make it like a paste and add it
    • Just add it to our tea
  6. Wait for some moment and and filer it in a cup and sip your tea
